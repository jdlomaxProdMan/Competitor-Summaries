Covariant Overview

Founded in 2017 by AI pioneers from UC Berkeley and OpenAI, Covariant specializes in AI software that allows robots to see, think, and act in complex warehouse environments.

  • Headquarters: Emeryville, California, USA
  • Website: covariant.ai
  • Leadership: Ted Stinson (CEO), Pieter Abbeel (Co-founder)
  • Status: Independent operation following a major 2024 licensing deal with Amazon.
